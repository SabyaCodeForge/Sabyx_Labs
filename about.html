Who We Are

A technology company built around craft and accountability.

Sabyx Labs is a technology company specializing in custom software, AI solutions, web platforms, mobile apps, and UI/UX design. We work with startups and established businesses alike, acting as an embedded technical partner rather than an outsourced vendor.

Every engagement is led by senior engineers and designers who take ownership of outcomes — not just tickets. We believe good software is a product of clear thinking, disciplined execution, and honest communication throughout.

Meet the Founder

Sabyasachi Mondal

Sabyx Labs was founded by Sabyasachi Mondal, a developer and UI/UX designer driven by a simple conviction: software companies should be judged by what they ship, not what they pitch.

With a hands-on background across web technologies, product design, and applied AI, Sabyasachi leads Sabyx Labs with a builder's mindset — staying close to the code, the design details, and the client relationship on every project.

"We started Sabyx Labs to build the kind of software we'd want to use ourselves — thoughtful, fast, and honest about trade-offs."
